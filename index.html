Tackling Hand Tremors One Meal At A Time

GYROTRAY

People

Over ten million people worldwide have Parkinsons.

Problem

Parkinsons cause hand tremors that make it difficult to perform daily tasks with independence.

Possibilities

GyroTray gives Parkinsons patients the ability to live their lives to a greater degree, opening their possibilities to live a normal life.

MORE ABOUT US

Our team is looking forward to a future where all Parkinsons patients can live completely independent lives and accomplish anything they want.

HOW WE WORK

The GyroTray utilizes state-of-the-art Arduino sensors and mechinization to create platforms to assist patients Starting with a stable platform.

Ideate

Use Computer Aided Design principles to mock up a tray.

Integrate

Edit test-trees in indicated sites then let it grow and reproduce, and then examine its offspring to see if it has any mutations.

Iterate

Repeate the design and building proccesses until we reach a smooth, user-friendly tray

Install

Combine the physical tray with software which uses AI to determine a level position.
