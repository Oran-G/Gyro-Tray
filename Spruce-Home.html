About the Issue

Deforestation Rates

For every 10 years of deforestation, 201 gigatons of carbon emissions are released into the atmosphere.

Our Solution

We plan to create faster-growing trees that can revitalize deforested ecosystems using directed evolution and gene editing.

Saving Our Planet

We can't wait to see how these new trees help achieve carbon neutrality, expand animal habitats, and restore the earth's natural beauty.

MORE ABOUT US

Our team is looking forward to a future where we can stop destroying our forests and begin to repopulate them.

HOW WE WORK

Spruce aims to utilize genetic editing and directed evolution to regenerate ecosystems and forests that have been laid to waste by deforestation.

The Tech

Use AI to recognize genomic sites impacting growth.

The Plan

Edit test-trees in indicated sites then let it grow and reproduce, and then examine its offspring to see if it has any mutations.

The Process

Grow test trees for multiple generations to test edit performance and durability using genome sequencing kits.

The Outcome

Repeat proccess until suitable gene line is established then we plan to deploy Spruce.
